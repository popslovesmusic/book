# Paper: Riemann Hypothesis as a Dynamic Fractal Vein Model Regulator

## Abstract

This paper demonstrates that the Riemann Hypothesis (RH) emerges as a natural stability condition within the Dynamic Fractal Vein Model (DFVM). We formalize the spectral properties of the fractal vein network, represented as a graph Laplacian, and show that the distribution of its eigenvalues is regulated by a trace formula analogous to the one for the Riemann zeros. This establishes a deep connection between number theory and the causal geometry of spacetime, where the RH acts as a fundamental constraint on the complexity and stability of the cosmic structure.
